The Cottage

You want the campfire.
Not the sleeping bag.

Colorado Bend Cottage sits right on the Colorado River, steps from the trails and swimming holes of Colorado Bend State Park and Post Oak State Park. It's a fully equipped 650 sq ft cottage with a 200 sq ft loft, a full kitchen, a private bathroom, and porches on both the front and back overlooking the river and canyon. You get the stars, the river sounds, and the limestone cliffs — without giving up the comforts of home.

Life on the River

Private river access with canoes and kayaks included for adults and kids. Swim, float, fish — your own stretch of the Colorado is waiting.

🥾

Wild Texas Outdoors

Walk directly into Colorado Bend State Park. Hike the canyon trails, discover Gorman Falls, and explore one of the most dramatic landscapes in central Texas.

Comfortable Base Camp

Full kitchen, private bath, a cozy loft, and front and back porches facing the river. Dog-friendly. Sleeps 8. Every modern amenity — just with better views.
